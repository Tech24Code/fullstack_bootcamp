The course is designed to be completed over a span of 7 months, with a commitment of approximately 5-7 hours per week.Each week includes video lectures,reading materials and assignments that help reinforce the concepts learned.While the course is self-paced, we encourage the participants to stay on track with the weekly schedule, to maximize their learning experience.

By the end of the course, you will have the skills to build several web applications.Examples include a "Personal Portfolio Website", that showcases your projects and skills; a dynamic "E-commerce Platform" with user authentication and payment integration, and a "real-time chat application" using WebSockets. Additionally, you will learn how to create a "task management tool" simillar to Trello, using ReactJS for the front-end, NodeJS and ExpressJS for the back-end, and MongoDB as the database.You would also be able to build small applications that integrate the third party APIs, such as a "weather dashboard" that fetches real-time data from a weather API. The final project will be a comprehensive application that incorporates all these skills,serving as a capstone to demonstrate your proficiency and creativity, providing you with a portfolio piece to show potential employers or clients...and much more.
